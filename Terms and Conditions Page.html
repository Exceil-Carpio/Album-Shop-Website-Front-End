ORDERS:

Updates:

  • Order updates will be provided through a master list attached to the order confirmation email to the buyer.

Order Modification:

  • Changing the album version is allowed until the pre-order is ongoing.
  • Reduction of orders can be accommodated while the pre order is ongoing. Later than that will not be entertained.

Cancellation of Orders:

  • Cancellation of orders are allowed, however, payment of 50% per album will be charged for the cancellation to push through. Failure to do so will be posted as a bogus buyer and will be blacklisted.

PAYMENTS:

Modes of Payment:

  • We are only accepting payments through our indicated modes of payment which are specified in the order confirmation email sent to the buyer. Payments that will be sent to others than the given modes will not be considered valid.

Proof of Payment:

  • Proof of payment should be attached to the form indicated on the order confirmation email. Payment confirmations will be sent 1-3 days after the payment and proof were received.

Late Payment Fee:

  • The shop is strict on compliance with the deadline of payment (DOP), thus failure to do so will get the buyer automatically charged Php. 30 per day. An email to notify this will be sent to the customer and it will also reflect on the master list.

DOP Extension Requests:

  • This will only be granted to buyers who will be able to give valid reasons with proof. However, this concern shall be addressed one day prior to the actual deadline for payment. Failure to do so will have the request denied.
